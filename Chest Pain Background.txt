# Chest Pain: Scope and Clinical Importance
::content::
## Epidemiology and Health Care Utilization
- Second most common ED presentation in U.S. adults (>6.5 million visits/year)
- Nearly 4 million outpatient visits annually
- Often noncardiac but requires careful evaluation

## Coronary Disease Context
- CAD affects >18.2 million U.S. adults
- Leading cause of death in men and women (>365,000 deaths/year)
- Chest pain is the most common presenting symptom
